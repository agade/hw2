I am from a Waverly, IA. It is a smaller farm town, but still has a lot of big city perks. I am the second of two kids. My parents are Dave & Becki. I have an older brother, Isaac, who is an Assistant Manager at a new Fareway store in West Des Moines.

In addition to the Hawkeyes, I am a die hard Yankees fan. I'm also quite the LeBron James fan as well (Even though I get a lot of criticism for it).
